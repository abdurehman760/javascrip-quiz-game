Basic Quiz Instructions

Total Questions

The Basic Quiz consists of [TotalCount] questions. For each quiz session, 10 questions are randomly selected and shuffled from the full set to ensure a varied experience.

Points System

  • 10 points for answering with 6 or more seconds remaining
  • 9 points for answering with 5 seconds remaining
  • 8 points for answering with 4 seconds remaining
  • 7 points for answering with 3 seconds remaining
  • 6 points for answering with 2 seconds remaining
  • 5 points for answering with 1 second remaining
  • 4 points for answering with 0 seconds remaining
  • 0 points for not attempting

Time per Question

You have 10 seconds to answer each question. If you do not answer within the time limit, the question will be skipped and no points will be awarded.

Bonus Time

Quicker responses will help you earn more points. Try to answer as soon as possible to get the highest score!

In addition, bonus time is awarded based on how quickly you answer each question. The faster your response, the more bonus points you receive, which can significantly boost your overall score.

Your total score is calculated based on the accuracy of your answers and the total time taken. Efficient answering contributes to a lower total time, which in turn improves your accuracy and overall score. Aim to strike a balance between speed and accuracy to achieve the best results!

What to Expect After the Quiz

After completing the quiz, you will see a comprehensive summary of your performance, including:

  • Total Score: Your overall score out of 100, reflecting your performance in the quiz.
  • Accuracy: The percentage of correct answers out of the total questions attempted, giving you an idea of your accuracy.
  • Total Time Taken: The total time you spent answering the questions, measured in seconds.
  • Remark: A performance remark based on your score, accuracy, and time taken. The remarks can range from 'Needs Improvement' to 'Excellent' depending on your results.

You will also be able to review each question and see which ones you answered correctly or incorrectly. For each question, you'll find:

  • Correct and Incorrect Answers: Highlighted options indicating your selected answer and the correct answer.
  • Time Taken: The amount of time you spent on each question.
  • Points Awarded: Points given for each question based on your response and timing.
  • Introduction: Any additional explanation or context provided for the question to help you understand the topic better.

If your new result is the best compared to your previous records, you'll see a special note indicating "New Record!" to celebrate your achievement.

Additionally, you can view all your previous quiz records, including the current quiz result, by clicking the "Show Previous Records" button. This will display a table of all your past results, allowing you to compare your performance over time and see how you have progressed. The table includes details such as total score, accuracy, time taken, and remarks for each quiz.

Project Purpose and Description

Project Purpose:

This project aims to develop a comprehensive and interactive quiz application designed to enhance users' knowledge and provide a fun learning experience. By incorporating features such as timers, question shuffling, and score tracking, the application seeks to create an engaging environment for both basic and advanced quizzes. Source Code: You can find the source code for this project on GitHub or Replit.

Project Description:

  • Dynamic Quiz Selection: Users can choose between basic and advanced quizzes, each with different difficulty levels and question types.
  • Interactive Quiz Interface: Questions are presented with multiple-choice options, and users can select their answers using radio buttons.
  • Real-Time Feedback: Instant feedback is provided after each answer, highlighting correct and incorrect choices and awarding points based on the time taken to answer.
  • Timer Functionality: The application includes a timer for each question and an overall timer for the quiz, with options to pause and resume the quiz.
  • Results and Analytics: At the end of the quiz, users receive detailed results, including accuracy, score, time taken, and a breakdown of answers.
  • Responsive Design: The application is designed to be user-friendly and accessible across different devices and screen sizes.
